Added the `plasmapy.formulary.fusion` module, which provides
`~plasmapy.formulary.fusion.cross_section` and
`~plasmapy.formulary.fusion.reactivity` for evaluating fusion cross
sections and Maxwellian-averaged reactivities for nine common fusion
reactions, including deuterium–tritium, deuterium–deuterium, and
deuterium–helium-3. Both quantities are computed from the Bosch–Hale
Padé (rational-function) parametrization of the astrophysical
S-function. For the reactions tabulated by Bosch and Hale (1992) the
published coefficients are used, while for the remaining reactions the
same functional form is fit to evaluated cross sections from the ENDF/B
library.
