The charged-particle-radiography `~plasmapy.diagnostics.charged_particle_radiography.synthetic_radiography.Tracker` now raises a clear `ValueError` when the source or detector is located at the grid origin, or when the source and detector are placed on the same side of the origin, instead of silently constructing the simulation with undefined (``NaN``/``inf``) plane-normal geometry or a meaningless magnification (see :issue:`2077`).
